Russian Stroganoff Recipe

Description

Russian Stroganoff is a creamy and flavorful dish made with tender strips of beef cooked in a rich sauce of sour cream, mushrooms, and onions. This hearty meal is typically served over egg noodles or rice, making it a comforting choice for dinner.

Ingredients

  • 500g beef (sirloin or tenderloin), cut into thin strips
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 200g mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon flour
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley (for garnish)

Directions

  1. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the onions and mushrooms, cooking until softened.
  2. Add the beef strips to the skillet and cook until browned.
  3. Sprinkle the flour over the beef and mix well. Gradually add the beef broth, stirring constantly until the sauce thickens.
  4. Remove the skillet from heat and stir in the sour cream. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with chopped parsley, over noodles or rice.
