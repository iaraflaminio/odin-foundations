Coconut Condensed Milk Tapioca Recipe

Description

Tapioca with coconut and condensed milk is a delightful Brazilian treat that's both sweet and satisfying. This easy-to-make dish features tapioca pearls filled with a creamy mixture of coconut and sweetened condensed milk, creating a deliciously chewy dessert that is perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup tapioca pearls
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/4 cup grated coconut (optional)
  • Water (for soaking)
  • Salt to taste

Directions

  1. Soak the tapioca pearls in water for about 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. In a saucepan, combine the coconut milk, condensed milk, and a pinch of salt. Heat over medium heat until warm, but do not boil.
  3. Add the soaked tapioca pearls to the saucepan and cook until they become translucent, stirring occasionally, about 10 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and let cool slightly. Serve warm or chilled, topped with grated coconut if desired.
